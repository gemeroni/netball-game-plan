Netball Game Planner - Guide

Selecting a round

Use the dropdown at the top to switch between scheduled games with sub headers showing available fixture information and rostered duties.

Assigning positions manually

Tap a quarter column header (Q1-Q4) to open the position bucket at the bottom. The bucket shows only unassigned positions for that quarter. Tap a position card to select it (it lifts), then tap a player row to assign it. Tap the position card again to deselect.

You can also tap directly on any cell in the grid to open the position picker for that player and quarter.

Auto-allocate

Tap AUTO to fill all empty slots. The algorithm balances positions across the whole season - players who have played a position least often get priority. Any positions you've pre-filled are left as-is.

  • QUARTER mode - each quarter is solved independently. Best for maximum rotation.
  • HALF mode - positions stay the same across each half (Q1=Q2, Q3=Q4 roughly). Only the sub player swaps mid-half. Good for players who need time to settle into a position.

In all modes, no player will sub more than once per game.

Clearing assignments

Tap a quarter tab (Q1-Q4) at the bottom to select it, then tap CLEAR Q# to wipe that quarter's assignments. With no tab selected, the button becomes CLEAR ALL and wipes every quarter at once. No confirmation - saves immediately.

Absences

Each player has a green tick (present) or red cross (absent) button. Tap to toggle. When a player is marked absent, their positions are automatically redistributed - to the fill-in player if available, or to Sub players. If 6 or fewer players remain active, WD is removed from the rotation and positions are recalculated.

Fill-in players default to absent. Tap the button to mark them as present for the game.

Player notes

Tap a player's name to open their options panel. You can add a note (e.g. "limit to sub - ankle") that is visible to you as coach context.
